Beware of fraud

Fraudsters may use the “National Alert” as an opportunity to mislead the public. If you receive a call from a private number claiming to be from government authorities such as Dubai Police, the Ministry of Interior, or the Ministry of Defense, and are asked for your Emirates ID, bank details, or any personal information, do not share these details under any circumstances.

Always verify requests through official government channels. Please do not delete your HSBC UAE app based on instructions from anyone else.
